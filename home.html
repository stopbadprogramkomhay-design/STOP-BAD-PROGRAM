Stand Together, Overcome the Pain

Join us in fighting bullying and discrimination through awareness, action, and compassion.

Definition

Bullying

Repeated aggressive behavior, intentional harm, or intimidation towards someone perceived as vulnerable.

Paulit-ulit na agresibong pag-uugali laban sa mahina.

Discrimination

Unfair or prejudicial treatment based on race, gender, age, religion, or disability.

Hindi patas na pagtrato batay sa lahi, kasarian, edad, relihiyon, o kapansanan.

Actionable Framework

S – Seek Support

Find a safe adult, teacher, or counselor immediately.

Humingi ng tulong sa ligtas na nakatatanda.

T – Tell the Truth

Report details of the incident to the Guidance Office.

Isalaysay ang buong pangyayari.
